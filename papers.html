Research Topics

Theoretical Foundations

Papers focusing on asymptotic properties, convergence rates, and theoretical frameworks for derivative estimation.

Methodological Developments

Works introducing new methods, algorithms, and computational approaches for derivative estimation.

Applications

Studies demonstrating practical applications of derivative estimation in various fields.

Methodological Approaches

Spline-based Methods

Including P-splines and penalized regression splines. Known for smooth estimates and efficient computation.

  • Eilers & Marx (1996, 2021)
  • Claeskens et al. (2009)

Kernel-based Methods

Traditional approach using kernel smoothing. Strong theoretical foundations.

  • Rice (1986)
  • Müller et al. (1987)

Local Polynomial Methods

Modern approach combining local fitting with robust estimation.

  • Wang & Lin (2015)
  • Kong et al. (2023)
