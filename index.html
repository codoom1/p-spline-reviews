Welcome

This website presents a comprehensive review of research papers focusing on non-parametric derivative estimation using penalized B-splines (P-splines). The research examines methods for selecting optimal smoothing parameters when estimating derivatives of regression functions.

Research Focus

Our work specifically addresses:

  • Asymptotic properties of P-spline derivative estimators
  • Development of improved methods for smoothing parameter selection
  • Theoretical analysis of convergence rates across different knot regimes
  • Practical implementation and finite sample performance comparisons

Browse through the reviewed papers to understand the theoretical foundations and recent developments in this field, or read about our contributions in improving derivative estimation methods.

Key Findings

Our research demonstrates that while existing smoothing parameter selection methods work well for mean function estimation, specialized approaches are needed for derivative estimation. We propose new methodologies that achieve optimal convergence rates while maintaining strong finite-sample performance.
